### Free Online Resources for TOEFL Preparation

Many schools require the Test of English as a Foreign Language (TOEFL) administered by ETS for graduate school applications. Typically, you don't have to take the test if you are from an English-speaking country or have done your (undergraduate) studies in one of those countries. 

The following provides a list of free online resources to study for the TOEFL test.

#### Resources from ETS

- [Prep course on edX](https://www.edx.org/course/toefl-test-preparation-the-insiders-guide)
- [Interactive mock test](https://www.edx.org/course/toefl-test-preparation-the-insiders-guide)
- [Practice sets](https://www.ets.org/toefl/test-takers/ibt/prepare/practice-tests.html)
- [Quick prep](https://web.archive.org/web/20190429133302/http://www.ets.org/toefl/ibt/prepare/quick_prep/)
